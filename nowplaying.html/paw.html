PAW PATROL: THE MIGHTY MOVIE
1h 33min
When a magical meteor lands in Adventure City, it gives the PAW Patrol pups superpowers, turning them into MIGHTY PUPPIES! For Skye (Mckenna Grace), the smallest dog on the team, her new powers are a dream come true. But things go south when the pups encounter their main enemy, Humdinger, who broke out of prison and teamed up with Victoria Vance (Taraji P. Henson), a mad scientist obsessed with meteors, to steal their superpowers and turn them into super villain. The fate of Adventure City hangs in the balance, the Mighty Pups must stop a supervillain before it's too late, and Skye needs to learn that even the smallest pup can make a big difference.
