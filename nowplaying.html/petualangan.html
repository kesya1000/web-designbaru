PETUALANGAN SHERINA 2
2h 6min
SHERINA (Sherina Munaf) and SADAM (Derby Romero), two childhood friends who were separated for a long time, meet again in Kalimantan for the release of orangutans. The sweet reunion was interrupted when a baby orangutan named SAYU was stolen by a group of people. Sherina and Sadam must try to find togetherness again, in order to save Sayu and also the continuity of their friendship which has just grown again. A sequel to the legendary film, musical nuances with a touch of action adventure.
