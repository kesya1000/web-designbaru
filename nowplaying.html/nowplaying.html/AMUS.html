AIR MATA DI UJUNG SAJADAH
1 Hr 54 Min
Seven years have passed, Aqilla (Titi Kamal) just found out that her child, Baskara (Faqih Alaydrus), is still alive and being raised by Arif (Fedi Nuril) and Yumna (Citra Kirana), a couple who only have one hope: to have a child. Aqilla departs from her empty life in Europe to the city of Solo to find her new future. Will Aqilla have the heart to take away the Baskara that Arif and Yumna have raised for years? Indeed, Aqilla's flesh and blood is in Baskara, but there is also Yumna's sweat and tears. Who has more right to be Baskara's mother?
