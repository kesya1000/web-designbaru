IMAM TANPA MAKMUM
1h 57min
IMAM (Syakir Daulay), a man who is looking for a makmum for his life, his name is imam but it is difficult to find a makmum, falls in love with a woman named NAIRA (Vonny Felicia), who is good at playing the violin, is the owner of a shelter for street children and has rosy cheeks. The Imam called her humaira, like the Prophet Muhammad's affectionate nickname for his wife Aisyah. MAMAK (Cut Mini) disagrees, apparently Humaira is a woman of the night.
